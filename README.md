## 百度百科教育叙事

**Baidu Baike - Educational Narratives**

- **Resource Type:** Encyclopedia/Reference Material
- **Language:** Chinese
- **URL:** [https://baike.baidu.com/](https://baike.baidu.com/)
- **Description:** Comprehensive encyclopedia resource providing definitions, theories, and historical context related to educational narratives (教育叙事). Useful for understanding foundational concepts and terminology.
- **Key Topics:**
  - Educational narrative theory
  - Narrative pedagogy
  - Teaching stories and case studies
  - Educational research methodologies

## SoundCloud账户

**SoundCloud Audio Resources**

- **Platform:** SoundCloud
- **Type:** Audio/Podcast Platform
- **URL:** [https://soundcloud.com/](https://soundcloud.com/)
- **Description:** Audio hosting and streaming platform where educators can share podcasts, lectures, and audio narratives about educational experiences.
- **Use Cases:**
  - Listen to educational podcasts
  - Access recorded lectures and talks
  - Discover audio narratives from educators
  - Share your own educational content

**Finding Educational Content:**
- Search for "educational narratives" or "教育故事"
- Follow educators and educational institutions
- Explore curated playlists on teaching methodologies

## Substack专栏

**Substack Newsletter Column**

- **Platform:** Substack
- **Type:** Newsletter and Written Content
- **URL:** [https://substack.com/](https://substack.com/)
- **Description:** Email newsletter platform where writers share articles, essays, and narratives. Ideal for publishing regular educational content, teaching reflections, and pedagogical insights.
- **Use Cases:**
  - Subscribe to educational newsletters
  - Read deep-dive essays on teaching and learning
  - Access author's archives and back issues
  - Engage with community discussions

**Benefits for Educational Narratives:**
- Direct audience connection
- Regular publication schedule
- Community engagement features
- Archived content library
- Easy sharing and subscription management
